Leaving The Netherlands

With Eefje Snel

The Netherlands is a great country to live in, but sometimes you just need an escape. My parents are real travelers and love to explore. They took me with them everywhere they went, so I've been around. Here you can see where I have already been and where I would love to go some day. You can read about my experiences and decide for yourself if you might experience the country yourself. Let's just leave The Netherlands already!
